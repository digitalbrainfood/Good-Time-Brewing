Non-Alcoholic IPA

A 6-pack of our signature non-alcoholic IPA, dry-hopped for easy sipping day through night. A variety of barley, wheat, and oat malts provide a well-rounded backbone upon which the hops can shine. Enjoy this healthy convergence of an East-coast and West-coast style IPA.
